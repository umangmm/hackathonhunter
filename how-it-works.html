Getting set up

  • Download the app

    Download the Wolfbeacon app on your iOS or Android devices.

  • Choose your platform

    When you sign up, you'll have two options: The Hacker Platform or The Organizer Platform. Choose the option that best matches your need.

    Sign up here.

  • Add or view hackathons

    Once you've signed up for Wolfbeacon, you'll be able to access nearby hackathons as well as the tools necessary to build your own hackathon.

  • See for yourself

    The sheer number of awesomeness WolfBeacon can bring to you is best experienced live. Sign up today and see for yourself.
